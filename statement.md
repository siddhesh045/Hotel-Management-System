1. PROBLEM STATEMENT
- Small hotels often struggle to manage room availability, customer check-ins, check-outs, and billing using manual methods, which can lead to errors, inefficiency, and poor customer experience. This project aims to develop a simple, console based Hotel management system that allows staff to manage rooms, store customer details, process bookings and generate bills efficiently.

2. TARGET USERS
- Small hotels with manual methods
- Hotels in small towns
- Lavish hotels who aim for good customer services

3. HIGH LEVEL FEATURES
- NO double booking
- Errors are handled gracefully in the code
- Proper check-ins and check-outs
- Detailed info about room numbers and its type
- Making the room available after the particular guest has checked out
